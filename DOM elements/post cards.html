Most career coaches don’t stall because they don’t get clients.
They do because they run out of time.

This is what it usually looks like:

First, you help your client find jobs.
You go on LinkedIn and Indeed, searching role by role, trying to find the right fit.

Then you organize everything.
Spreadsheets, Notion, Docs.

Tracking where they applied, what version of the resume they used, and what stage they’re in.

Then comes execution.
You check in daily or weekly.

Did they apply? Did they network? Did they follow up?

Then you try to diagnose what’s wrong.
Are they not getting interviews?
Is it a resume problem?
A positioning problem?
A networking problem?

But most of the time, you’re guessing because you’re relying on what your client tells you and not actual data.

So you go back.
You update the spreadsheet. You adjust the strategy. You tweak the resume.

Manually. Every time. For every client.

And if you’re doing this for 5 clients, it’s manageable.
Try doing it for 15.

That’s where most coaches hit the wall.
Because the process doesn’t scale.

That’s the problem we kept seeing.
So we asked a simple question:

What would this look like if it didn’t depend on you doing everything manually?

That’s where CoCareer comes in.

It does what you’re already doing, just without the chaos.

Instead of searching manually, it aggregates the right opportunities
Instead of spreadsheets, everything is tracked in one place
Instead of guessing, you see what’s actually happening
Instead of chasing updates, it’s all there in real time

So you can stop managing the process and go back to what actually matters:
Coaching. Thinking. Guiding. Helping people win.

The best coaches shouldn’t spend their time tracking work.
They should spend their time creating impact.

That’s what we built this for.
